\section{Limitations and Scope}

The theory has one explicit approximation: the broadband surrogate. Accordingly, the surrogate-to-ODE derivation is exact only conditioned on that surrogate, and the scaling law \(\tau^*(L)=d_{\mathrm{head}}/\sqrt{L}\) remains an empirical law / conjecture rather than an unconditional theorem.

Empirically, the primary evidence is still multi-seed text extrapolation. The \(750\mathrm{M}\) continue result is single-seed supporting evidence, its broader downstream comparison is incomplete because of a dataset-download failure, and the video temporal results remain supportive rather than co-primary. We also do not elevate short-step LoRA or instruction-tuning evidence, because it confounds positional adaptation with task adaptation.
