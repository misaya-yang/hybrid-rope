\section{Experiments}
\label{sec:experiments}

\subsection{Setup}

All core experiments train from scratch with fixed architecture, modifying only the RoPE inverse-frequency initialization. We use $b{=}500\mathrm{K}$, the predicted $\tau^*(L)$ for each training length, and standard GPT-style decoder stacks \citep{radford2019gpt2} with $d_{\mathrm{head}}{=}64$ across 50M--750M. The primary datasets are TinyStories, FineWeb-Edu, and controlled passkey-mix settings; evaluations target PE-diagnostic metrics (extrapolation PPL, passkey retrieval, NIAH, downstream NLL) that isolate positional encoding quality from model capacity. Primary claims use 3~seeds per configuration. Full hyperparameters are in the supplementary material.

Evidence is organized in three tiers. \emph{Primary} (3-seed, directly tied to central claims): EVQ$\times$YaRN composition (\S\ref{sec:exp-yarn}), PE-dominant extrapolation (\S\ref{sec:exp-pe}), and MLA (Appendix~\ref{sec:mla-results}). \emph{Robustness} (3-seed, single architecture): capability preservation (\S\ref{sec:exp-robust}). \emph{Supporting} (1--3 seed): multi-scale PPL, progressive training, video DiT, LoRA at 7B (\S\ref{sec:exp-supporting}). No primary claim rests on tier-3 evidence.

\subsection{Primary result: EVQ unlocks YaRN}
\label{sec:exp-yarn}

Figure~\ref{fig:evq-yarn} and Table~\ref{tab:evq-yarn} present the paper's main systems result. The decisive comparison is EVQ+YaRN against Geo+YaRN---the question is whether the training-time frequency substrate affects inference-time scaling.

In the 454M 10\% mix setting (3~seeds per configuration), EVQ+YaRN reaches $100\%$ passkey retrieval at $8\mathrm{K}$ while Geo+YaRN remains at $61\%$; the same combination lowers PPL from $82.9$ to $70.9$ at $8\mathrm{K}$ and from $157.7$ to $107.5$ at $16\mathrm{K}$. EVQ alone improves over Geo without saturating retrieval, so the jump to $100\%$ under YaRN is not a ceiling effect. The composition reflects the orthogonal deficiency structure: EVQ corrects shape, YaRN corrects range, and their corrections are additive (Figure~\ref{fig:orthogonal}).

Table~\ref{tab:phase11-leverage} quantifies the interaction: YaRN provides only $-4.5\%$/$-3.1\%$ on Geo ($4$/$8\mathrm{K}$) but $-32.5\%$/$-40.7\%$ on EVQ ($\tau{=}4$). NTK-aware scaling becomes destructive at large~$\tau$, confirming complementarity requires preserving the optimized frequency structure.

\subsection{Primary result: PE-dominant extreme extrapolation}
\label{sec:exp-pe}

Figure~\ref{fig:pe-dominant} and Table~\ref{tab:pe-dominant} provide the second principal anchor, isolating frequency allocation from model capacity. In DAPE-style $128{\to}8\mathrm{K}$ extrapolation, EVQ achieves $333.7$ PPL ($-35\%$ vs.\ Geo baseline $513.7$), surpassing DAPE ($455.3$) with zero extra parameters. At $L{=}256$, the predicted $\tau^*{=}4.0$ is preferred across both $125\mathrm{M}$ and $454\mathrm{M}$---scale changes extrapolation difficulty but not the preferred allocation.

\subsection{Robustness and capability preservation}
\label{sec:exp-robust}

Multi-scale raw extrapolation (Table~\ref{tab:multiscale}, Appendix) shows in-range PPL changes within ${\pm}1.7\%$ while long-range PPL improves by $10$--$46\%$ across 50M--750M. A base-generalization sweep (454M, single seed) confirms EVQ leads at both $b{=}10\mathrm{K}$ and $b{=}500\mathrm{K}$, with EVQ's extrapolation PPL stable across base values ($192.4 \approx 191.9$) while Geo diverges by 38~points. Capability evaluation (Table~\ref{tab:capability}, Appendix~\ref{sec:supporting-experiments}) confirms EVQ is non-destructive when retrieval is absent.

\subsection{Supporting evidence}
\label{sec:exp-supporting}

EVQ generalizes beyond MHA. On MLA with only 16 RoPE channels (432M, 3-seed), EVQ alone reduces $2\times$ PPL by $31.1\%$ and outperforms Geo+YaRN---the strongest single-result evidence that allocation quality dominates when channels are scarce (Appendix~\ref{sec:supporting-experiments}). Post-training LoRA ($r{=}64$) into LLaMA-3-8B-Instruct \citep{grattafiori2024llama3} confirms effectiveness at 7B ($8$--$19\times$ PPL reduction at $16$--$32\mathrm{K}$, 300 steps). Progressive training (454M, single seed) shows EVQ+YaRN advantage widening from $-34.6\%$ to $-81.2\%$, and video DiT (2~seeds) confirms the collision mechanism operates across modalities. EVQ's effect attenuates through successive abstraction layers (PPL~$\to$~NLL~$\to$~retrieval~$\to$~QA) but never reverses. Full results in Appendix~\ref{sec:supporting-experiments}.
