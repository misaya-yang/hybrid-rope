\section{Experiments}

\subsection{Setup}

All core experiments are from-scratch and keep the model architecture fixed. We use \(b=500\mathrm{K}\), the predicted \(\tau^*(L)\) for each training length, and modify only the RoPE inverse-frequency initialization. The primary datasets are TinyStories, FineWeb-Edu, and controlled passkey-mix settings; the main long-context evaluations are retrieval-style extrapolation and broader suites such as LongBench and RULER when available \citep{bai2024longbench, hsieh2024ruler}. Architecturally, the models follow standard GPT-style decoder stacks \citep{radford2019gpt2} with $d_{\mathrm{head}}{=}64$ across all scales (50M--750M), and no extra positional network is trained.

Primary body claims use three seeds (raw PPL, passkey mix) or six seeds (EVQ\(\times\)YaRN retrieval). Evaluation splits retrieval-style localization from stricter AR-exact and multi-needle variants. Full hyperparameters and appendix-only baselines are in the supplementary material.

\subsection{EVQ unlocks YaRN}

Figure~\ref{fig:evq-yarn} and Table~\ref{tab:evq-yarn} contain the paper's main systems result. The decisive comparison is not EVQ against geometric RoPE in isolation, but EVQ combined with YaRN against Geo+YaRN. In the fair \(10\%\) mix comparison, EVQ+YaRN reaches \(100\%\) retrieval at \(8\mathrm{K}\) across the three seeds used in that setting, while Geo+YaRN remains at \(61\%\); the same combination lowers PPL from \(82.9\) to \(70.9\) at \(8\mathrm{K}\) and from \(157.7\) to \(107.5\) at \(16\mathrm{K}\).

EVQ alone improves over Geo without saturating retrieval, so the sharp jump to $100\%$ under YaRN is not a ceiling effect, and the PPL reductions confirm genuine long-range quality improvement. The composition reflects the orthogonal deficiency structure described in \S\ref{fig:orthogonal}: EVQ addresses shape, YaRN addresses range, and their corrections are additive.

\subsection{Extreme extrapolation and scaling-law confirmation}

Figure~\ref{fig:pe-dominant} provides the second principal anchor. In DAPE-style extreme extrapolation ($128{\to}8\mathrm{K}$), EVQ beats learnable PE with zero extra parameters ($333.7$ vs.\ $455.3$ PPL), isolating frequency allocation as the decisive variable. The $L{=}256$ Phase~11 confirmation shows that the predicted $\tau^*{=}4.0$ is preferred across both $125\mathrm{M}$ and $454\mathrm{M}$---scale changes extrapolation difficulty but not the optimal allocation.

\subsection{Robustness and capability preservation}

Table~\ref{tab:multiscale} summarizes the multi-scale pattern: EVQ pays at most a small in-range cost (\({\leq}1\%\)) while improving long-range PPL by \(10\text{--}46\%\) across 50M--750M scales. Capability-oriented evaluation (350M, 3-seed) confirms two properties: EVQ is non-destructive when retrieval is absent from training, and improves extrapolated retrieval by \(+10\text{--}13\mathrm{pp}\) when retrieval is learned. The base generalization sweep (454M, single seed) confirms EVQ leads at both \(b{=}10\mathrm{K}\) and \(b{=}500\mathrm{K}\), with EVQ's extrapolation PPL nearly identical across base values (\(192.4 \approx 191.9\)) while Geo diverges by 38~points---suggesting that frequency optimization partially compensates for suboptimal base choices.

Downstream QuALITY QA ($n{=}2086$, 454M, single seed) shows a signal gradient: EVQ's advantage is largest in PPL ($-30\%$ at $8\mathrm{K}$), preserved in Gold-Answer NLL ($-30\%$), and detectable in retrieval ($+13\mathrm{pp}$), consistent with model capacity---not PE---being the binding constraint on accuracy.

\paragraph{Progressive training widens EVQ's advantage.}
Three-stage progressive training (454M, \(512{\to}1024{\to}2048\), single seed) reveals that EVQ's advantage grows monotonically (Table~\ref{tab:progressive} in Appendix): the EVQ+YaRN PPL advantage at \(16\mathrm{K}\) widens from \(-34.6\%\) to \(-81.2\%\), driven by Geo+YaRN degradation ($3.80 \to 13.17$) while EVQ+YaRN remains stable ($2.48 \to 2.21 \to 2.48$). At Stage~3, EVQ+YaRN extends functional context to \(48\mathrm{K}\) (\(24\times\) training length) at PPL~\(2.63\). Strikingly, after the second progressive stage, EVQ \emph{raw} (PPL $11.2$ at $16\mathrm{K}$) surpasses EVQ+YaRN at the previous stage ($16.8$), indicating that continued training can \emph{internalize} the allocation benefit, making inference-time scaling redundant.

\paragraph{Multi-head Latent Attention.}
EVQ extends to MLA (the attention mechanism in DeepSeek-V2/V3), where only 16 frequency channels carry positional information. On a 432M model (3-seed), EVQ alone reduces \(2\times\) extrapolation PPL by \(31.1\%\) and outperforms GEO+YaRN at \(16\mathrm{K}\); EVQ+YaRN establishes strict dominance at every extrapolation length. Under thorough training (1B tokens), EVQ's raw advantage reverses ($+11.1\%$), yet EVQ+YaRN after fine-tuning still wins ($-2.5\%$)---a $13.6\mathrm{pp}$ structural reversal confirming that the frequency substrate is the binding constraint even when raw extrapolation saturates. Full results are in Appendix~\ref{sec:mla-results}.

\paragraph{Post-training injection via LoRA.}
To test whether EVQ transfers to pretrained LLMs, we inject EVQ-Cosh frequencies ($\tau{=}1.414$, the $\tau^*$ predicted for $d_{\mathrm{head}}{=}128$, $L{=}8192$) into LLaMA-3-8B-Instruct and fine-tune with LoRA ($r{=}64{=}K$, $\alpha{=}128$) on LongAlign-10k for 300 steps. The rank is set at the phase-transition threshold $r_c{=}d_{\mathrm{head}}/2$ predicted by our theory (\S\ref{sec:lora-regime}).

Table~\ref{tab:lora-8b} shows that EVQ-LoRA pays a modest in-distribution cost ($+30\%$ PPL at $8\mathrm{K}$) but reduces extrapolation PPL by $8\times$ at $16\mathrm{K}$ and $19\times$ at $32\mathrm{K}$, confirming that EVQ's frequency reallocation transfers post-hoc provided $r \geq r_c = K$. Generation-based evaluations (RULER) do not improve, indicating that LoRA capacity suffices for positional adaptation but not instruction-following at extrapolated lengths.

\paragraph{Signal gradient across abstraction layers.}
Aggregating across experiments, EVQ's effect attenuates through successive abstraction layers but never reverses: raw PPL $-52\%$ (next-token, Table~\ref{tab:multiscale}) $\to$ Gold-Answer NLL $-30\%$ (task-specific confidence, 454M QuALITY $n{=}2086$) $\to$ passkey retrieval $+60\mathrm{pp}$ (exact localization, 6-seed) $\to$ QA accuracy $+2.2\mathrm{pp}$ (argmax at capacity floor, $p{\approx}0.02$). This monotone attenuation is the expected signature of an infrastructure-level improvement: PE allocation sets a ceiling that downstream metrics inherit with progressively coarser resolution.
