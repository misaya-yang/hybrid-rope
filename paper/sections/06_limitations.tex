\section{Limitations and Scope}
\label{sec:limitations}

The variational derivation is exact conditional on the broadband surrogate $K_{\mathrm{app}}$; the practical $\tau^*$ rule is semi-analytic: the $d_{\mathrm{head}}$ factor, $L^{-1/2}$ exponent, and $\chi^2$ stiffness are derived, while the remaining $\mathcal{O}(1)$ scale is fixed by normalization convention and falls inside the observed flat PPL basin. The causal evidence is PE-diagnostic (extrapolation PPL, passkey, NIAH, gold-answer NLL); MMLU, HumanEval, RULER, and QA are useful non-regression checks, not the causal scope of the paper. From-scratch ${\geq}1\mathrm{B}$ text validation and production MLA at $b{=}10\mathrm{K}$ remain future work; LoRA, progressive training, video DiT 382M, and MLA 1B-token composition are supporting boundary checks. The video DiT $0.53\times$ correction is post-hoc rationalization, and a principled modality-aware $\tau^*$ is future work.
