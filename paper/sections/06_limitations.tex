\section{Conclusion and Limitations}
\label{sec:conclusion-limitations}

RoPE allocation \emph{shape}---not only frequency \emph{range}---is a third design axis; EVQ-Cosh ($\tau^*{=}d_{\mathrm{head}}/\sqrt{L}$) is a closed-form zero-parameter substrate that downstream methods inherit, not a replacement for them. Limitations: PE-diagnostic endpoints (PPL, passkey, NIAH/RULER, gold-NLL) are causal targets, with downstream accuracy as a non-regression check; theory is exact conditional on $\mathcal{C}_{\mathrm{app}}$, while $\tau^*$ is semi-analytic ($d_{\mathrm{head}}$, $L^{-1/2}$ derived; $\mathcal{O}(1)$ prefactor by convention). From-scratch ${\geq}1\mathrm{B}$ text and production MLA ($b{=}10\mathrm{K}$) are natural follow-ups.
