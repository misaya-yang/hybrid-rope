\section{Conclusion and Limitations}
\label{sec:conclusion-limitations}

RoPE allocation \emph{shape}---not only \emph{range}---is a third design axis; EVQ-Cosh ($\tau^*{=}d_{\mathrm{head}}/\sqrt{L}$) is a closed-form zero-parameter substrate downstream methods inherit. Theory is exact conditional on $\mathcal{C}_{\mathrm{app}}$; $\tau^*$ is semi-analytic ($d_{\mathrm{head}},L^{-1/2}$ derived; $\mathcal{O}(1)$ prefactor by convention). Production MLA, ${\geq}1\mathrm{B}$ from-scratch, and per-channel-method head-to-heads are natural follow-ups.
