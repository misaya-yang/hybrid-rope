\section{Limitations and Scope}

\paragraph{Theory-to-practice gap.} The variational derivation is exact conditional on the broadband surrogate $K_{\mathrm{app}}$. The practical $\tau^*$ rule is semi-analytic (\S\ref{sec:tau-star}): $d_{\mathrm{head}}$, $L^{-1/2}$, and the $\chi^2$ stiffness are derived; the $\mathcal{O}(1)$ Lagrange multiplier is fixed by unit convention ($\lambda{=}1$ vs.\ collision-oracle $1.171$, ${\approx}17\%$ offset inside the $<1\%$ PPL basin).

\paragraph{Training-time method; PE vs.\ capability adaptation.} EVQ modifies frequency initialization before training: below the critical rank $r_c{=}d_{\mathrm{head}}/2$, coupling stiffness between pretrained weights and geometric frequencies makes redistribution infeasible (Appendix~\ref{sec:lora-regime}). Zero-shot replacement on Qwen2.5-7B-Instruct \citep{qwen2024qwen25} yields $-0.35\%$ LongBench (not significant); LoRA at $r{=}r_c$ recovers $8$--$19\times$ extrapolation-PPL gains at $+30\%$ in-distribution cost (Table~\ref{tab:lora-8b}). RULER/QA do not improve at extrapolated lengths---LoRA repairs PE-attributable degradation but cannot substitute for task-specific patterns never trained at those lengths.

\paragraph{Scale and evaluation.} From-scratch experiments span 50M--750M (text), 129M--382M (video DiT), and 432M (MLA); LoRA extends to 7B; ${\geq}1\mathrm{B}$ from-scratch is deferred. MLA uses $b{=}500\mathrm{K}$; production MLA ($b{=}10\mathrm{K}$) requires validation. The progressive, video DiT 382M, and MLA 1B-token composition runs are single-seed. Evaluations target PE-diagnostic metrics; capability benchmarks (MMLU, HumanEval) are untested.

\paragraph{Modality-dependent correction for video DiT.} On bidirectional DiT the empirically optimal $\tau$ is ${\approx}0.53\times$ the causal-attention rule's prediction; a post-hoc decomposition attributes ${\approx}1/\sqrt{2}$ to the doubled relative-position range and the residue to diffusion-noise attenuation, but we treat this as rationalization rather than derivation. A principled modality-aware $\tau^*$ remains future work (Appendix~\ref{sec:tau-correction}).
