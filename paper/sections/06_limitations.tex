\section{Limitations and Scope}

\paragraph{Theory-to-practice gap.} The variational derivation is exact conditional on the broadband surrogate $K_{\mathrm{app}}$, validated across 12 configurations (Appendix~\ref{sec:surrogate-validation}). The practical $\tau^*$ rule is semi-analytic: $d_{\mathrm{head}}$ dependence and the $L^{-1/2}$ exponent are derived, but the proportionality constant $\lambda$ requires calibration. The exact-kernel collision-score optimum yields $\lambda_{\mathrm{coll}} \approx 1.17$; the deployed formula uses $\lambda{=}1$, which falls inside the flat PPL basin ($<1\%$ gap from per-configuration optima across 27 configurations). Closing $\lambda$ analytically remains open.

\paragraph{Training-time method, not zero-shot plug-in.} EVQ modifies the frequency initialization before training. Zero-shot replacement on Qwen2.5-7B-Instruct \citep{qwen2024qwen25} (21-task LongBench, bootstrap, FDR correction) yields $-0.35\%$ overall (not significant): retrieval gains are offset by multi-hop QA losses. Even 300 steps of LoRA adaptation ($r{=}d_{\mathrm{head}}/2$) recovers $8$--$19\times$ extrapolation gains at a $+30\%$ in-distribution PPL cost (Table~\ref{tab:lora-8b}), establishing a practical deployment path where extrapolation quality is prioritized. The variational analysis predicts a phase transition at $r_c{=}d_{\mathrm{head}}/2$; RULER scores do not improve, indicating LoRA suffices for positional adaptation but not instruction-following at extrapolated lengths.

\paragraph{Scale and evaluation.} From-scratch experiments span 50M--750M (text) and 129M--382M (video DiT); LoRA extends to 7B. Full from-scratch training at ${\geq}1\mathrm{B}$ is deferred. The MLA experiment uses $b{=}500\mathrm{K}$; production MLA deployments (DeepSeek-V2/V3) use $b{=}10\mathrm{K}$, and the effect at lower base values requires validation. The progressive result, video DiT 382M scale-up, and MLA 1B-token composition are each single-seed (supporting evidence). All evaluations target PE-diagnostic metrics (extrapolation PPL, passkey retrieval, downstream NLL); standard capability benchmarks (MMLU, HumanEval) are not tested, so EVQ's impact on downstream task accuracy remains open.
