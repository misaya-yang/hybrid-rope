\section{Limitations and Scope}
\label{sec:limitations}
\vspace{-0.5\baselineskip}

Our claims are about a PE mechanism, not production long-context SOTA: causal evidence comes from PE-diagnostic endpoints (extrapolation PPL, passkey, NIAH/RULER, gold-answer NLL), and downstream accuracy at these scales mixes positional quality with semantic capacity and is only a non-regression check. The theory is exact conditional on $\mathcal{C}_{\mathrm{app}}$; $\tau^*$ is semi-analytic, with $d_{\mathrm{head}}$ and $L^{-1/2}$ following from small-$\tau$ scalings and the $\mathcal{O}(1)$ prefactor fixed by convention inside a flat basin. From-scratch ${\geq}1\mathrm{B}$ text, production MLA at $b{=}10\mathrm{K}$, tuned EVQ+LongRoPE2, and a principled RF utility exponent are natural follow-ups; 1--2-seed supporting evidence extends scope but does not anchor primary claims. Training-time allocation \emph{shape} emerges as a RoPE design axis complementary to operator and range.
