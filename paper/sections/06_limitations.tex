\section{Limitations and Scope}
\label{sec:limitations}
\vspace{-0.5\baselineskip}

Our claims target a PE mechanism, not production long-context SOTA: causal endpoints are PE-diagnostic (PPL, passkey, NIAH/RULER, gold-answer NLL); downstream accuracy is a non-regression check only. Theory is exact conditional on $\mathcal{C}_{\mathrm{app}}$; $\tau^*$ is semi-analytic, $d_{\mathrm{head}}$ and $L^{-1/2}$ structural, $\mathcal{O}(1)$ prefactor by convention. From-scratch ${\geq}1\mathrm{B}$ text, production MLA at $b{=}10\mathrm{K}$, and tuned EVQ+LongRoPE2 are natural follow-ups.
