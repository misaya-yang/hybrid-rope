Theoretical Pivot & Exact Variational Quantization (EVQ)

1. The Narrative Pivot: Broadband Decomposition as the Main Theorem

Problem in Previous Draft: Relying on the "Diagonal Surrogate" as the primary objective function introduced an unexplainable ~11% approximation error and failed to explain the structural necessity of preserving mid-band frequencies, leading to the use of an arbitrary heuristic (Anchored-Sigmoid).

The Pivot: We elevate the Broadband Decomposition (previously Appendix F.1) to the Main Theorem. In the broadband limit ($b, L \to \infty$), the full phase-interference kernel exhibits strict scale separation, decomposing exactly into a singular diagonal ridge and a smooth off-diagonal Brownian covariance:


$$K(\phi_1, \phi_2) \xrightarrow{b, L \to \infty} \alpha \delta(\phi_1 - \phi_2) + \beta \min(\phi_1, \phi_2)$$


Where $\alpha$ represents the local discrete energy ridge, and $\beta$ represents the global cross-frequency coupling.

Taking the variation of the full regularized functional yields the exact ODE:


$$\alpha \rho''(\phi) - \beta \rho(\phi) = 0, \quad \rho'(1)=0$$


Exact Solution:


$$\rho^\star_{\text{full}}(\phi) \propto \cosh\left(\tau(1-\phi)\right), \quad \tau = \sqrt{\frac{\beta}{\alpha}}$$


Insight: The ~11% error was never a defect of the proxy; it is the precise physical manifestation of the off-diagonal Brownian covariance ($\beta$). The global coupling naturally smooths the density, deriving a strictly convex ($\rho'' > 0$) profile without relying on manual anchor thresholds.

2. The Fallacy of Sigmoid and Heuristic Warps

The community relies on parametric curves (like YaRN or our previous Sigmoid proxy) to map frequencies. However, Sigmoid functions contain inflection points (拐点) where the second derivative changes sign.
When applied to frequency allocation, an artificial inflection point creates a "dead-zone" in the mid-band ($\phi \approx 0.3 \sim 0.6$), drastically compressing local resolution coordinates. This topological violation is the root cause of catastrophic forgetting in mid-range Multi-hop reasoning tasks (e.g., Musique).

3. Exact Variational Quantization (EVQ)

To faithfully deploy the theoretical continuous optimum to discrete attention heads without introducing topological artifacts (inflection points), we introduce Exact Variational Quantization.

Using the exact Inverse-CDF of the Cosh profile:


$$F_{\cosh}(\phi) = \frac{\int_0^\phi \cosh(\tau(1-t)) dt}{\int_0^1 \cosh(\tau(1-t)) dt} = 1 - \frac{\sinh(\tau(1-\phi))}{\sinh(\tau)}$$


Inverting $F(\phi_k) = u_k$ yields the absolute closed-form warp equation:


$$\phi_k = 1 - \frac{1}{\tau} \text{arcsinh} \Big( (1 - u_k) \sinh(\tau) \Big)$$


Mathematical Elegance:

Zero Heuristics: The parameters $(a, \kappa, c)$ are eliminated. The only parameter $\tau$ is a physical ratio representing the relative strength of global covariance vs. local ridge.

Topological Isomorphism: The mapping is strictly convex, preserving local resolution in the mid-band.

Strict Degradation: By Taylor expansion, as $\tau \to 0$ (no extrapolation tension needed), the equation mathematically reduces to $\phi_k = u_k$, recovering standard Geometric RoPE exactly.
